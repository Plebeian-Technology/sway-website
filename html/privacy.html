Welcome to the LegisMe Privacy Policy

Below we are going to outline how we collect information from you and what we do with it.

How does LegisMe collect information from me?

When you use LegisMe, we collect information that you enter and store it in a database on the Google Firebase platform.

If you would like to view this information, please schedule a privacy walkthrough by clicking the Support ? bubble in the bottom-right corner of the LegisMe app at app.legisme.org.

What does LegisMe do with my information?

Information you enter into LegisMe is only used for presenting your information back to you such as displaying what legislation you have voted on and how you compare to your reprensentatives through a LegisMe Score.

What information does LegisMe collect?

We only collect information you give us. We do not collect analytics on how you use LegisMe and we do not show ads.

Does LegisMe sell my data?

No.

Will LegisMe ever sell my data?

LegisMe will not. However, with your explicit permission, we may offer a feature where you can sell your data in the future to interested parties. We would likely take a small percentage of any proceeds.

Can I delete my LegisMe account?

You may request an account deletion by emailing legis@legisme.org or during a privacy webinar.

How do I know you're being honest?

LegisMe offers privacy webinar/walkthroughs over video chat where we can show you what the LegisMe application and database looks like. If you attend a webinar and give us permission to view it, we can show you your account data.

You can register for a privacy walkthrough by clicking the Support ? bubble in the bottom-right corner of the LegisMe app at app.legisme.org.

There aren't ads?

Never.
